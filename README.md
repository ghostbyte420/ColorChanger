## Color Changer
**A powerful Windows Forms application for advanced color substitution and image manipulation**

---

## **Features**

✅ **Precision Color Replacement** – Replace specific colors with pixel-perfect accuracy

✅ **Real-Time Preview** – Instant visual feedback as you adjust settings

✅ **Advanced Interpolation** – Multiple modes including NearestNeighbor (default), Bilinear, and Bicubic

✅ **Smooth Navigation** – Zoom and pan with Ctrl+Left Click (pan) and Middle Click (reset)

✅ **Image Transformation** – Horizontal mirroring with one click

✅ **32-bit ARGB Support** – Professional-quality color handling

✅ **Adjustable Threshold** – Fine-tune color replacement sensitivity

✅ **Multiple Format Support** – Save and load BMP/PNG images
